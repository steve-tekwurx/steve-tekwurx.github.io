uControl Documentation 5.0 : uAdmin

Created by Steve Hicks on Jul 08, 2016

This section covers how to upgrade uControl using admin panel.

 1) Login to uControl:

 

2) After logging in- from the dashboard navigate to uAdmin-System-Update
3) Enter your unique reference id-given to you by uControl administrator
4) Hit Update button
5) You will be logged out and will be asked to login again.
6) when you login you can see your uControl is updated to the required version.
