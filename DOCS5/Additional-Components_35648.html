uControl Documentation 5.0 : Additional Components

Created by Steve Hicks on Jul 08, 2016

 

Based on the already linked software products (Products, Services, Packages or Filesystems) uControl will try and identify any other products that may also make up part of your application.

Individual

To add a single software product to your application:

  1. Click on the row and select OK on the popup

 

 

Multiple

To add all or multiple software products to your application:

  1. Select the tick box on the far left side of the table row and click the 'Include Selected' button
