uControl Documentation 5.0 : Exporting Policy Breaks

Created by Steve Hicks on Jul 08, 2016

uControl allows the ability to export the policy break information.

To Export the data for a selected policy navigate to uPolicy:

  1. Select 'View' to view the policy breaks
  2. Select 'Download as CSV' to download the results to CSV format
